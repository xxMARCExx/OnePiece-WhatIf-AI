What if Luffy recruited Nami first instead of Zoro? Without a swordmaster to anchor him, he drowns facing Arlong alone in the East Blue. The crew never forms—Usopp and Sanji refuse to join a captain who died chasing an impossible dream. Zoro becomes the world's strongest swordsman anyway, but serves a Yonko as his right hand. The sea takes the boy with the rubber smile, and the throne of the Pirate King sits empty, waiting for a darkness that no one will ever challenge.
