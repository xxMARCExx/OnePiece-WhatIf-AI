# What If Luffy Never Met Zoro What if Luffy had sailed with only Nami, and faced Arlong without a swordsman at his side? Beaten and broken, a captain without strength cannot hold his crew together. Sanji walked away, Usopp followed, and Nami disappeared into the fog. Alone, the boy who dreamed of becoming Pirate King stumbled into Loguetown as nothing but a ghost. Standing before Gold Roger's last legacy, Luffy didn't claim the throne—he burned it, understanding finally that the sea demands more than will. The age of pirates ended where it began, with an empty beach and the silence of a dream that drowned.
